Mini Project — Annual Sales Report
Project Question:
You are a data analyst at a national trading company.
Your manager wants a complete 12 month sales
performance report with full analysis.

Task 1 — Complete the table using formulas:
1. Total Revenue = Units Sold × Unit Price
2. Commission = Total Revenue × 5%
3. Net Revenue = Total Revenue - Commission

Task 2 — Create summary in Sheet 2:
1. Total orders — COUNT
2. Total revenue — SUM
3. Total commission paid — SUM
4. Total net revenue — SUM
5. Highest single order — MAX
6. Lowest single order — MIN
7. Average order value — AVERAGE
8. Total units sold — SUM

Task 3 — Answer these questions manually:
1. Which month had highest total revenue?
2. Which salesperson earned most revenue?
3. Which category sold most units?
4. Which region had highest net revenue?
5. Which product appeared most in orders?
